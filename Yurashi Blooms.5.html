The Story

Hi, I'm Yashu — the hands behind every bloom.

What started as a quiet creative hobby — twisting pipe cleaners into tiny flowers — slowly bloomed into something I never expected: a small business built entirely on love and craftsmanship.

Every bouquet and keychain from Yurashi Blooms is made by me, by hand, one piece at a time. I pour care into every petal and make sure each creation is exactly what my customer imagined — often even better.

My goal is simple: to create something that lasts, captures a feeling, and brings a smile — not just today, but every time you look at it.
